Established in 1968, George Brown College (GBC) offers students a combination of theory and 
hands-on experience, prioritizing real-world learning. 
The college offers more than 160 career-focused educational paths across diploma, 
certificate, postgraduate, and degree levels, developed with substantial input from industry experts 
to ensure relevance and currency. GBC's faculty, enriched with real-world industry experience, is committed to 
imparting practical knowledge and skills to students.


The Athletics Division strives to provide a variety of high-quality sports programs and recreation services that meet the diverse needs of 
George Brown students in an accessible and inclusive way.
